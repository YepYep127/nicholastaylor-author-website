Essaerist War

They march for conquest. They fight for survival. But war answers to no one.

Book One

Ash and Ruin

Blood turns the ground to mud. Smoke blots out the sky. The war drums of Lysandrian echo across a world on the edge of conquest.

For generations, the Ulfgarath have raided the frontier, carving their names into legend with steel and fire. But now, the empire marches — not for retribution, but for annihilation. Caught between them are the Essaerists, rare magic-wielders who can shape reality itself. The invasion has begun. The old world is burning. And what rises from the ashes may never be the same.

Book Two

Cinder and Sorrow

Ashes fall like snow. The crumbled walls of fortresses rise like tombstones, unable to protect the slain. The war that scorched the frontier now spreads like a storm across the heartlands.

Lines once drawn in blood begin to blur — between conqueror and protector, justice and revenge, duty and defiance. In the silence between battles, some seek shelter. Others, absolution. But the fires of empire burn on, consuming not just cities, but the very truths people once held sacred.

And across the land, those who wield the power to shape the world must ask: What is worth preserving… and what must be turned to cinders?
