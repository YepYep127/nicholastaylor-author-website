The Service Term Series

In a distant future where humanity has conquered aging, the looming threat of interstellar war perpetually hangs over the Human Federation.

Book One

Extermination on Pike Prime

Young Alex Taylor is about to begin his compulsory term of military service in the Human Federation Defense Force. Deployed to the alien world of Pike Prime, Alex will face impossible choices in a gritty, action-packed, and deeply human story of war and survival.

Book Two

Wasteland Patrol

Set on the desolate world of Lepus 328rb, Alex and his team are assigned to assist the Human Federation Exploration Corps. Gritty, pulse-pounding, and deeply human — the war on the frontier is far from over.

Book Three

The Last Drop

After five years of service, Alex and his team find themselves at a crossroads — facing the most advanced and dangerous enemy yet. The series reaches its powerful conclusion in a story about duty, sacrifice, and what it means to come home.
